I'm a computer scientist, deeply interested in computer security and privacy.
I have loved the art of computer programming since childhood, and I strive to dedicate
a significant portion of my life to contributing to open-source software.

During the day, I work in information security as part of a Computer Emergency Response
Team (CERT). My role lets me collaborate on exciting projects, respond to incidents,
and learn alongside an [incredible team](https://www.circl.lu/team).  
My [resume](/files/resume_cedric-bonhomme_web-version.pdf) is available if you'd like to know more.

I [contribute](/software) to various open-source projects, mainly — but not only — in the infosec field.
My favorite programming languages are Python and OCaml.

Outside of computers, I'm passionate about [photography](/photography); I have loved it
as far back as I can remember, and received my first camera when I was 8 years old.
I play the piano a lot, and I read books — though not as much as I would like.
I also enjoy running in nature.
